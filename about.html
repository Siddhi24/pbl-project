About Us



We have developed this website to help both oldies and housewives too!
This website will gather the housewives together who deliver homemade tiffins to the oldies at very low costs.
This website helps the people not only with the homemade tiffins but also gives the ladies to sell their homemade papads,pickles or whatever food items they make!
The old people can also get help with the medicines they want or maids for their daily house chores.

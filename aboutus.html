About Us

The main objective of our website is to provide a platform to those women or men who do not have the space or capital to start a mess.
They can register on this website and sell their homemade tiffins very easily.
Students who live far from home for their career and education will get better home cooked meals
They will be served a meal made with fresh vegetables and less oily meals.
This will help those people in need of money. At the same time students,elderly people who live far away from their families will get this home cooked meal delivered at home.
